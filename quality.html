Quality & Compliance

Global Barite follows strict quality control procedures to ensure API-grade Barite with consistent performance for oil & gas and industrial applications.

Our Quality Philosophy

Quality at Global Barite is not limited to final inspection. It is embedded across mining, processing, testing, and dispatch to ensure reliable and repeatable product performance.

Quality Control Process

01

Raw Material Selection

Barite ore is sourced from captive mines with controlled extraction and grade monitoring.

02

Processing Control

Crushing, grinding, and micronization parameters are monitored to achieve required particle size.

03

Laboratory Testing

Each batch undergoes laboratory testing for specific gravity, particle size, and chemistry.

04

Batch Approval

Only batches meeting API and customer specifications are approved for packing and dispatch.
